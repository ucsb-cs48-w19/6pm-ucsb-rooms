WhereIsEvery1?
  • About Us

We're a small team students based at UCSB, who want to help you find your classes quickly.
With the power of this search engine, look for any classroom by entering the building
number and the room number, and check out it's schedule. That way, you can see if
it's free and open to study in. Long term this project is looking to expand to find
new ways to help students plan and schedule.

Stay tuned for more updates!
